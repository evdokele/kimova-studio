About
Watch with sound

I started Kimova Studio because I kept finding myself drawn to the part that comes before the animation — the concept, the motion language, the rules that make everything hold together. Seven years of freelance across agencies, direct clients, VR projects, and more explainer videos than I can count. At some point I wanted a studio that reflected how I actually think about the work.

I work with SaaS companies and tech products on launches, feature videos, UI walkthroughs — the full motion layer behind a product. The goal is always to make the product feel as good as it actually is.

I'm based in Italy and work with clients across Europe and the US. New tools are part of how I work — the landscape keeps moving and I move with it.
