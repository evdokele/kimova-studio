Kora

Personal finance platform · 2025
Watch with sound
The Challenge

Kora is a personal finance platform that replaces the anxiety of not knowing with a single number — your Financial Health Score. Launching in a crowded fintech market meant the challenge wasn't explaining features, it was communicating a fundamentally different idea: not another budgeting app, but a product that tells you the truth about your money simply and without judgment.

The Approach

The hero video was built around a single emotional arc: fog → clarity → confidence. Rather than leading with UI, the opening lives in abstraction — fragmented numbers and transaction lines drifting across a near-black frame, evoking that familiar low-level financial uncertainty. Kora doesn't announce itself loudly. It arrives as resolution. The visual language — deep dark backgrounds, a violet-to-cyan gradient, sharp geometric type — was designed to sit comfortably alongside Revolut and Linear: premium, intelligent, never cold.

The Result

A 30-second launch film that positions Kora as the fintech product that finally makes your money make sense.
