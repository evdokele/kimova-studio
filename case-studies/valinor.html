The Challenge

Valinor is a pioneering biotech using machine learning to build virtual patient models — simulating how therapies perform before a single human trial begins. Launching out of stealth in early 2025, they needed a bold, clear statement of purpose to attract both talent and investors. The brief was to translate a genuinely complex scientific mission into something immediately felt — not explained.

The Approach

The launch film had to do two things simultaneously: communicate hard scientific credibility and inspire an emotional response. The approach was to lead with mission and vision rather than technology — letting the animation carry the weight of what Valinor is working toward, with composited live action footage grounding it in the human stakes of the work. A second film followed: a live action and animation piece introducing the C-suite leadership as the team built toward a Series A raise, balancing the rigour of a biotech announcement with the warmth of a founding story.
